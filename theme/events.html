DhiEva was officially registered as an NGO under The Societies Registration Act 1860 on August 23rd, 2023. DhiEva opened an office with a counseling facility at Room no101, KSM Archade, DPO Road, Palakkad-14

Activities

DhiEva is committed to imparting sessions for motivating and guiding students at school and college levels for maintaining a stable mental health while focusing their future goals. The following sessions were conducted during the tenure;

1. Govt Girls HS School Aalathur- 19-9-23 Motivation

2. Mind Matters Mercy college – 30-10-23

3. Nuel Public School, Vilayodi – Effective Parenting Session Nov 11th 2023

4. ASMMHSS Aalathur – Lifeskill, Nov 20th 2023

5. Sree Krishna HSS Nallepilly, Feb 27th 2023- Adolescent Physical and Mental health Awareness

6. “Stress Management of Women at Workplace and Home” for women employees during KSFESA, Vanitha Convention, March 3rd,2024

7. Adolescent Health club, ICDS of Thrithala Block, March 8th 2024 - Online session on Life Skill
