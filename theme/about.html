DhiEva was officially registered as an NGO under The Societies Registration Act 1860 on August 23rd, 2023. DhiEva opened an office with a counseling facility at Room no101, KSM Archade, DPO Road, Palakkad-14

Our Vision

To become a proactive change agent by educating people to strengthen their mental wellness and develop an inclusive and sustainable society with humanitarian considerations.

Our Mission

To upskill society to be progressive in thought and action, sensitize the younger generation, heal the inner mind, support vulnerable people in society and assist individuals to design their life.

Aims and Objectives

1. To promote Mental wellness, Quality Education, Social Equity, Sustainable development, Human rights and Child Rights.

2. To facilitate assessment and provide need-based counselling for children, Adolescents, Women & LGBTQ.

3. To increase awareness among children and women on their rights, social and civic sense.

4. To build the capacity of women and children in understanding and availing the welfare facilities provided by the State.

5. To advocate and represent issues related to children, women and transgender community.

6. To defend women, transgender and children from all types of discrimination and violence within society and communities.

7. To prevent and protect vulnerable women, transgender and children from sexual abuse, exploitation, and trafficking.

8. To work in collaboration with the Ministry of Women and Child Development Government of India, Ministry of Human Resource Development Government of India, the Department of Women and Child Development Government of Kerala, and any other local bodies, state or central government agencies/departments and private agencies for the progress or welfare of women, children and transgenders.

9. To provide Student counselling, Mental Health counselling, Pre-marital counseling, Couple counseling, Family counselling, Divorce and Post-divorce counselling, Geriatric counselling.

10. To organise and conduct Seminars and Training programs on life enrichment for students, personal transformation, soft skills, motivation, women empowerment, entrepreneurship, Mental wellness, environmental awareness, geriatrics, rural and urban development etc

11. To organise a club for women to encourage interaction, recreation, community intervention, social service and feeling of oneness.

12. To Mobilize resources, promote rural and urban leadership, and encourage economic independence among women and the Transgender community.

13. To help identify small-scale income-generating initiatives for vulnerable women, especially those with disabilities, single mothers, refugees, women leading single life and transgenders, thereby improving their livelihood

14. Increase opportunities for professional and personal transformation of women and transgender through skill support and guidance.

15. Contribute and take positive steps to protect human rights, participate in the development process, peacebuilding and enhance access to justice.

16. To receive financial assistance (in the mode of loan or otherwise) from government, Non-government organisations, Banks, any other legal entity, individuals or groups on reasonable terms and conditions.

17. To accept donations, grants, presents and other offerings from individuals, groups or other organisation ( in the form of movable or immovable properties) which shall be utilized to achieve the objectives of the society.

18. To purchase/acquire land for different establishments and to construct building thereon for the cause of society

19. To provide socio-economic support to poor and needy people in the community.

20. To provide physical and mental support for senior citizens through house visits. conduct and coordinate recreation and informative programs for them.

21. To collaborate with Medical or allied agencies for conducting camps, awareness programs for senior citizens.

22. To collaborate with Central and State governments, Private organizations, Individuals or groups to create environmental awareness programs and implement projects.

23. To protect natural resources and promote sustainable development.
